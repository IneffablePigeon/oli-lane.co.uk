Computer Science

I'm an undergraduate pursuing a BA in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge. My skills include a strong grounding in Java, C#, Javascript and ML, and some experience with Python and PHP.

My course involves a first year which is 50% CS, 25% Maths and 25% Physics. The remaining two years are 100% CS.

I enjoy both the theory and practical sides of Computer Science. Some of the areas I'm most interested in include Algorithms, Software Engineering, Cryptography and Machine Learning.

My second year group project was based on creating an experimental system to enable chat phrases in a game to be quickly built up from a basic grammar, restricting possible messages to valid sentences to speed up input.

In my spare time I enjoy tinkering with web technologies, mobile apps and games programming.
